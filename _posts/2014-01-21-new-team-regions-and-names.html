As I described in my previous post, Basketball GM needed some new cities and team names. Thanks to your great suggestions, we narrowed it down to a short survey that got over 100 responses (thank you to featherfooted for setting it up). You can view the final survey results here.

However, as the commissioner of the league, I decided to make a few minor executive decisions:

  • Voters picked the New York Skyline over the Bankers by just one vote, but I went with Bankers because I like it a lot better.
  • Voters picked the Atlanta Peaches over the Gold Club by just five votes, but I went with Gold Club because I like it a lot better.
  • Voters picked the San Francisco Hippies over the Entrepreneurs or the Venture Capitalists, but I think that was mainly because the latter two options are similar and they split the vote. So I went with Venture Capitalists (even though it's kind of too long).
  • Voters picked Symphonies for the name of the Seattle team, but I changed it to Symphony because I think the singular form sounds better.

In total, that leaves these new teams:

  • Atlantic: Boston Massacre, Montreal Mounties, New York City Bankers, Philadelphia Cheesesteaks, Toronto Beavers
  • Central: Chicago Whirlwinds, Cincinnati Riots, Cleveland Curses, Detroit Muscle, Pittsburgh Rivers
  • Southeast: Atlanta Gold Club, Baltimore Crabs, Miami Cyclones, Tampa Turtles, Washington Monuments
  • Southwest: Dallas Snipers, Houston Apollos, Mexico City Aztecs, Phoenix Vultures, St. Louis Spirits
  • Northwest: Denver High, Minneapolis Blizzards, Portland Roses, Seattle Symphony, Vancouver Whalers
  • Pacific: Las Vegas Blue Chips, Los Angeles Earthquakes, Sacramento Gold Rush, San Diego Pandas, San Francisco Venture Capitalists

Before you ridicule my abuse of power, I should say that I also wanted to pick the Boston Three Party and Toronto Cosmopolitans, but they didn't even come close to winning and I couldn't bring myself to so blatantly rig the outcome.

The new team names are live now (only in new leagues). Please let me know if there are any problems. Thank you!
